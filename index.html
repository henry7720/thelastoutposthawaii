The Last Outpost Hawaiʻi is Hawaiʻi's Sci Fi and Fantasy Club. We meet regularly and support local conventions and host other events such as informal gatherings and movie showings. If you're a fan of science fiction and live on Oʻahu, contact us and stop by. Join us on Facebook.
